Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to rent a porta potty?

Standard units range from $80–$150/week. Luxury trailers cost $800–$2,500/day. Final pricing depends on location, duration, and service frequency—especially in high-demand states like Texas or Florida.

How far in advance should I book portable toilets?

For standard units, 3–7 days is usually sufficient. For luxury or trailer restrooms—especially in California or New York during peak season—book 2–4 weeks ahead.

Do you offer ADA-compliant portable restrooms?

Yes. All our service areas—including in Illinois, Arizona, and around Georgia—include ADA-accessible units with wider doors, handrails, and level flooring.

How often are portable toilets serviced?

Standard service is once per week for 10+ uses/day. High-traffic events may need 2–3 cleanings weekly. We customize schedules based on your needs nationwide.

Are luxury portable toilets worth it for weddings?

Absolutely. They enhance guest experience with real toilets, sinks, mirrors, and climate control—critical for outdoor weddings in Florida, Texas, or California.
